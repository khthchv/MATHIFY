Volume Calculators

Calculate the volume of different three-dimensional figures.

Cube

Formula: V = s³

Rectangular Prism

Formula: V = l × w × h

Cylinder

Formula: V = πr²h

Cone

Formula: V = (1/3)πr²h

Sphere

Formula: V = (4/3)πr³

Pyramid

Formula: V = (l × w × h) ÷ 3

Volume Formula Guide

Cube

All sides of a cube are equal in length.

Rectangular Prism

Multiply the length, width, and height.

Cylinder

Multiply the circular base area by the height.

Cone

A cone's volume is one-third of a cylinder with the same base and height.

Sphere

The volume depends only on the radius.

Pyramid

Multiply the base area by the height and divide by three.
