Perimeter Calculators

Find the perimeter or circumference of common geometric figures.

Rectangle

Formula: P = 2(L + W)

Square

Formula: P = 4 × Side

Triangle

Formula: P = a + b + c

Circle

Formula: C = 2πr

Parallelogram

Formula: P = 2(a + b)

Trapezoid

Formula: P = a + b + c + d

Perimeter Formula Guide

Rectangle

Add the length and width, then multiply by two.

Square

Multiply one side by four.

Triangle

Add the lengths of all three sides.

Circle

The perimeter of a circle is called the circumference.

Parallelogram

Add one base and one side, then multiply by two.

Trapezoid

Add all four sides together.
