Area Calculators

Calculate the area of common geometric figures quickly and accurately.

Rectangle

Formula: A = Length × Width

Square

Formula: A = Side²

Triangle

Formula: A = ½ × Base × Height

Circle

Formula: A = πr²

Parallelogram

Formula: A = Base × Height

Trapezoid

Formula: A = ((a + b) × h) ÷ 2

Area Formula Guide

Rectangle

Area is calculated by multiplying the length and width.

Square

The area is the square of one side.

Triangle

Multiply the base and height, then divide by two.

Circle

Multiply π by the square of the radius.

Parallelogram

Area equals base multiplied by height.

Trapezoid

Add both bases, multiply by the height, then divide by two.
